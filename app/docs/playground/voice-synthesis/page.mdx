## Eburon Voice Synthesis

Create natural, expressive voice audio with Eburon Voice v1.

### Features (Preview)

- **Multiple Voice Profiles**: Choose from emotionally diverse, neutral, or specialized voices
- **Emotional Expression**: Control stability and expressiveness
- **Audio Tags**: Breathing, pauses, and micro-noises for natural delivery
- **Multi-Speaker**: Generate dialogue with different voices
